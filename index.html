Our Ceo

Ceo's Message

With gratitude for divine guidance, Margaret Danso founded our company in 2000 with a vision to blend innovation and sustainability.
Starting with modest resources, her leadership has transformed the business into a leader in real estate, logistics, animal farming, cocoa farming, and water supply.
Through perseverance and dedication, Margaret continues to drive our commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.


Our Vision:

To create sustainable and innovative solutions that positively impact communities, while setting new standards of excellence across diverse industries.


Our Mission:

To deliver exceptional value through innovative design, sustainable practices, and a commitment to quality, ensuring long-term success for our clients, partners, and the environment.

Areas of Focus

Our Operational Sectors

Animal Farming

Our animal farming operations in the Eastern Region and Greater Accra Region at Dawhenya, Ghana focus on sustainable and humane practices. We prioritize the welfare of our animals, ensuring they are raised in a healthy and safe environment. Our farms are designed to minimize environmental impact, promoting eco-friendly methods that preserve the natural resources of the region.

Total Area

1.2 Acres

Cocoa Farming

Our cocoa farming operations are situated at Akyem Abomosu in the Eastern Region of Ghana. We focus on sustainable and responsible farming practices to ensure the highest quality cocoa beans.

Total Area

20 Acres

Water Supply

We supply borehole water to farming communities, ensuring a consistent and reliable flow of clean water. This initiative not only supports the agricultural needs of these communities but also contributes to the overall health and well-being of the people living there.

Total Area

2900 Sq. Ft

Logistics

Located at Tema freezone, our logistics services ensure efficient and timely delivery of goods and materials to support the development and maintenance of our projects. We also provide inter-city transportation services.

Real Estate

We specialize in building and leasing affordable, economically-sized housing projects. Our projects are designed to meet the needs of a wide range of individuals and families, providing comfortable and sustainable living spaces.

Total Area

4 Acres
